Your Strategic Staging Partner

We partner with real estate professionals throughout Temecula and Southern California to elevate listings, attract buyers, and help homes sell faster and for higher value

At Aviara Design Co.

we understand that every listing is an opportunity

Our approach to home staging is both strategic and refined, designed to enhance a property’s marketability while aligning with your timeline and goals. Whether you’re preparing a vacant home or an occupied listing, we provide tailored staging solutions that help your listings stand out in a competitive market.

We proudly serve Temecula, Murrieta, Menifee, Fallbrook, San Diego, Orange County, and the Inland Empire.

Our Process
Consultation

We assess the property and determine the most effective staging approach based on layout, condition, and target buyer.

Proposal

You receive a customized quote outlining recommended services, scope, and next steps.
